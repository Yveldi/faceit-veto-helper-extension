# Privacy Policy

**Faceit Veto Helper** is designed to collect nothing and send nothing. All
processing happens locally in your browser.

## What the extension does

- It runs only on `faceit.com`.
- It reads match and player information from FACEIT's own API, using your
  existing logged-in session (the same data already visible to you on the
  site). These requests go directly from your browser to FACEIT; the extension
  adds no server of its own.
- It saves your preferences (auto-accept on/off and delay, veto helper on/off,
  regret helper on/off) in the browser's local extension storage.

## What it does not do

- It does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.
- It does **not** send data to the developer or to any third party.
- It contains **no** analytics, tracking, or advertising.
- Nothing the extension reads ever leaves your browser.

## Permissions

- `storage`: to remember your settings on your own device.
- Host access to `faceit.com`: so the helper can run on the matchroom pages and
  call FACEIT's API with your session, same-origin.
